Social login refers to using familiar Web2 authentication providers such as Google, Steam, etc., and email to onboard Web3 users. Though it eliminates the friction of setting up and  managing keys for blockchain transactions, a third party may have the custody of user's keys.

This is where Arcana social login makes a difference. Our solution is secure, non-custodial  where users retain privacy and full ownership of keys. 

**Arcana social login** makes it super easy for users to onboard applications and instantly access the embedded, non-custodial, embedded Arcana wallet to sign blockchain transactions.
